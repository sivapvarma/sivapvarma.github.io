For any $x \in \mathbb R$, we have

$$1 + x \le e^{x}. $$

To see this, observe that $e^x$ is a convex function and $1+x$ is a tangent to $e^x$ at $x=0.$ By a change of variable, $x = \log y$ we have $\log y \le y-1$ for any $y \ge 0.$
